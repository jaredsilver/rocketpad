Philosophy

OVERVIEW

    The RocketPad Program curriculum and all six respective modules were developed and specifically adapted for young entrepreneurs with consideration of multiple methodologies and frameworks and with a focus on action and practice. The RocketPad Program covers the following modules sequentially:

IDEATE

  • Design Thinking / Ideation / Product Developments
    • Understanding the multiple startup methodologies
    • Completing our custom ideating process for new ideas
    • Understanding the product development process

FORMULATE

  • Business Models & Customer Validation / Professionalism
    • Learning how business models work and choosing appropriately
    • Identifying, targeting, and interviewing specific customer archetypes
    • Focusing on how to be young and professional concurrently

WORK

  • Structure / Finance & Accounting / Basic Legal / Marketing & Sales
    • Learning the legal implications of being a high school entrepreneur
    • Crafting marketing, pricing, and sales strategies with consultation
    • nderstanding basic bookkeeping and reporting obligations

MANAGE

  • Operations & Business Management for Young Entrepreneurs
    • Understanding how operations work for individual companies
    • Learning how to manage school and employees as a young entrepreneur
    • Completing our leadership and management training for youths

GROW

  • Tangible Growth / Scalability & the Future
    • Learning how to grow operationally, financially, and physically
    • Understanding scalability as a concept for individual businesses
    • Considering the implications of traditional schooling/college path

COLLECT

  • Pitching to an Investor Audience / General Business Communications
    • Completing training on pitching actual business or idea to real investors
    • Understanding different types of investments available and which to take on
    • Learning communication strategies for partners, customers, and investors
