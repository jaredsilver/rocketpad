Overview

    The RocketPad Program curriculum and all respective modules were developed and specifically adapted for young entrepreneurs with consideration of multiple methodologies and frameworks and with a focus on action and practice. The RocketPad Program covers the following modules sequentially:

Design Thinking & Ideation

  • Learn about multiple startup methodologies
  • Complete our custom ideation process
  • Understand traditional product development systems

Business Models & Professionalism

  • Learn how different business models work and how to choose one appropriately
  • Identify, target, and interview specific customer archetypes
  • Focus on how to be both young and professional

Legal, Finance, & Sales

  • Learn the legal implications of launching a company in high school
  • Craft marketing, pricing, and sales strategies
  • Understand basic bookkeeping (GAAP) and tax reporting

Management & Ops for Young Entrepreneurs

  • Understand how operations work for individual companies
  • Learn how to manage school and employees as a young entrepreneur
  • Complete our custom leadership and management training

Growth & Scalability

  • Learn how to grow operationally, financially, and physically
  • Understand scalability as a concept for individual businesses
  • Consider the implications of traditional schooling/college path

Raising Capital & Pitching to Investors

  • Complete training on pitching actual business or idea to real investors
  • Understand different types of investments available
  • Learn how to take on investments and communicate with investors
