Founders

Nick Abouzeid

Nicholas Abouzeid is a four-time founder in the education, ecommerce, and retail food industries. As a senior at Harvard- Westlake, he raised a round from the school to develop and run HW Inc, a sold-out educational boot camp for students to explore entrepreneurship and learn the steps from idea to pitch. He currently studies as a Presidential Scholar at Babson College concentrating in Legal Studies and as a visiting scholar in the Wellesley College Education Department.
