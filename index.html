Do you take any equity from the accepted companies?

Nope! Any and all funding you receive from RocketPad comes with no strings attached. You’ll own 100% of your company at the end of the program.

Do I need to have a business idea prior to applying?

You do not need to have a business idea or plan! Everybody has passions, and we’ll work with you during the first week to transform yours into a scalable venture.

What is the advantage of applying early?

We review applications on a rolling basis, so the quicker you apply, the quicker you’ll hear back (and have a better shot at getting in!).
